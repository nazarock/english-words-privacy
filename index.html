English Words App — Privacy Policy & Support

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the "English Words" mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in using email or third-party services (e.g., Apple ID, Google), we store your basic account information (e.g., name, email).
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage data to improve app performance and features.
  • Progress & Preferences: We store your progress, vocabulary lists, and learning preferences to personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your user profile.
  • To sync your progress across devices (if supported).
  • To personalize vocabulary recommendations and content.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve our features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase) for authentication, analytics, or crash reporting. These providers follow their own privacy practices.

4. Data Retention

Your personal data and learning progress are stored securely as long as your account remains active. You may request account deletion at any time.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.
